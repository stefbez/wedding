Travel


Parking, Travel & Taxi's

There is ample parking at the venue and your car can be left overnight, they just need to be collected between 8am-11am the following day.

Please use the postcode SL6 3AD to find the venue.

If you are drinking and need to get to the hotel, there are taxi companies recommended by the venue.

U Want Taxis Telephone: 01628 622110

Riviera Cars Telephone: 01628 666777


Hotels

Manor Lane, Berkshire, Maidenhead SL6 2RA
